GUIDED TOUR AT THE MET CLOISTERS

Gardens of The Met Cloisters

SUNDAY / AUGUST 12

11:00 A.M.–12:00 P.M.

Offered daily through the end of September, these tours of the gardens at The Met Cloisters—the branch of the Museum devoted to the art and architecture of medieval Europe—include horticultural, architectural, and historical information.

Garden tours are offered at 11 am, daily. Tours are limited to 10 participants; meet in the Main Hall and depart promptly. For questions and tour availability, ask at the Admissions Desk.
